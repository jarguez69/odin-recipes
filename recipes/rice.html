Fried Rice

Delicious recipes

Fried rice is a great way to use up leftovers; it is infinitely variable, and there are plenty of variables worth testing out.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked, cold rice (day-old rice works best)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil (vegetable, canola, or peanut oil)
  • 2–3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2–3 green onions, sliced
  • 2–3 tablespoons soy sauce

Steps

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the beaten eggs and cook until scrambled. Remove them from the pan.
  3. Cook the garlic for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  4. Add the carrots and peas, then stir-fry for 3–4 minutes until tender.
  5. Add the cold rice and stir well to break up any clumps.
  6. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil, then mix until the rice is evenly coated.
  7. Return the scrambled eggs to the pan and stir everything together.
  8. Add the sliced green onions, season with salt and pepper, and cook for 1 more minute.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
