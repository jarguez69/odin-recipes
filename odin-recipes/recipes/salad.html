Epic summer salad

Delicious recipes

An epic summer salad is the perfect barbecue side dish as it requires no cooking, only assembling fresh summer produce.

Ingredients

  • 400g black beans drained
  • 500g heritage tomatoes chopped into large chunks
  • 500g ½ cucumber halved lengthways, seeds scooped out and sliced on an angle
  • 1 mango peeled and chopped into chunks
  • 1 large red onion halved and finely sliced
